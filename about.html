About

My Story

As a photographer and image editor, I’ve always been fascinated by how seemingly disconnected elements can come together to create unexpected beauty. patterns and themes are everywhere, but sometimes they need a little nudge to come out and shine.

Over the years, I found the same trend in how the teams and studios I worked with and managed operated: A beautiful workflow, a brilliant solution are sometimes just a little tinkering away. There’s nothing as satisfying as uncovering the perfect pattern just by asking the right questions and see where they lead.

That’s how I design: keeping eyes and ears open to help people and products realise their best selves.
